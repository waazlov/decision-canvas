# DecisionCanvas Demo Script

## Demo Setup

- Dataset: `data/ecommerce_demo.csv`
- Question: `Why did conversion drop last month?`

## 0:00-0:20 Problem Framing

"A lot of teams have business data, but they still lose time turning it into a clear decision. They can upload a CSV into a BI tool, but they still have to profile it, choose charts, interpret what changed, and package it for leadership. DecisionCanvas compresses that workflow into one product."

## 0:20-1:30 Live Product Walkthrough

"I’ll start in the workspace. First, I upload a CSV with e-commerce performance data. DecisionCanvas immediately profiles the dataset, detects likely metrics like revenue, orders, and sessions, identifies dimensions like device and region, and flags any quality issues."

"Next, I ask a business question in plain language: 'Why did conversion drop last month?'"

"When I generate the dashboard, the system runs deterministic analysis, surfaces the strongest finding, chooses the right chart type, and assembles an executive-ready view."

"At the top are the KPI cards, so I can immediately see the business impact. Below that, the findings section tells me what changed in plain English. The chart shows the trend behind the drop. On the right, the executive summary explains what likely happened, and at the bottom I get concrete next actions."

## 1:30-2:30 Why It Is Different

"The important difference is that this is not a chatbot and it is not a static dashboard. DecisionCanvas is a structured decision engine."

"It does not just answer with text. It profiles the data, runs deterministic analysis first, generates structured findings with confidence levels, maps those findings into approved visualization templates, and produces a dashboard that is ready to share."

"That makes it more useful for real teams, because the output is readable, explainable, and presentation-ready. Instead of giving you raw analysis, it gives you a decision artifact."

## 2:30-3:00 Closing Impact

"The value is speed and clarity. In a few clicks, DecisionCanvas turns raw data into a business narrative with KPIs, evidence, and recommended actions. For teams without full analytics infrastructure, that means faster decisions, cleaner communication, and less time lost between data and action."
